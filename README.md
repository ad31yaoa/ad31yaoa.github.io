# Product Requirements Document (PRD)
**Project:** "English Easy" English learning website
**Owner / Author:**  Adelya Meymanbekova
**Date:** 2025-11-11
**Version:** 1.0

---

## 1. Overview

**Website Name:** English Easy
**Purpose:** To help people all over the world learn english online in a fun and insighful way. Help students prepare for IELTS/TOELFL exams.
**Target Audience:** Students arund the ages 15 to 25

## 2. Goals
- Allow users to easily book a lesson or contact the teachers.
- Offer a free level test for new learners.
- Display other students' reviews.
- Provide helpful FAQs for new students.  
- Make the site layout look visually engaging and intuitively simple.

## 3. Features

1. **Home Section**  
   - “Start Learning” and “Book a Lesson” buttons.
   - Banner showing student success moments

2. **About Section**  
   - Short story about the creators(Adelya's) background.
   - Personal photo.

3. **Services Section**  
   - Private Lessons
   - Group Lessons
   - Exam Preparation (IELTS/TOEFL)
   - Business English 

4. **Level-test Section**  
   - 15-question quiz testing grammar and vocabulary.
   - Results page shows student level
   - Suggestion to book a lesson based on the result.

5. **Reviews Section**  
   - Real success stories   
   - Star ratings and country flags for international students.
   - “Leave a Review” button for current students.

6. **FAQ Section**  
   - General   
   - Payments
   - Scheduling
   - Learning Tips

7. **Contact Section**  
   - Email and direct message option.
   - Links to social media
   - Option to schedule a free trial lesson.  

8. **Footer**  
   - Quick navigation links.
   - Social media icons.  
   - Copyright notice.

## 4. User Stories
- **As a student**, I want to understand what types of lessons are offered before I decide to book.
- **As a returning student**, I want to leave a review to share my experience.
- **As a busy professional**, I want to schedule a lesson online quickly and easily.
- **As a social media user**, I want to connect with the teacher via social media for updates and tips.

## 5. Design 
- **Brand aesthetic** Minimal, light pink aesthetic with clean typography.  
- **Color Scheme:** Pale yellow #FFF3B0, peach-pink #FFD6C2, light lavender-pink (#E8B3FF), Light purple #A388E8 .
- **Typography:** ZFor headings: Lora, for body: Nunito.  
- **Navigation:** Top menu bar with links to About, Services, Level-test, reviews, contacts.  
- **Layout:** Simple, single-column scrolling pages with sections clearly separated.  
- **Images:** Photos of successful students.  

## 6. Technical Stack / Tools

- HTML5, CSS
- Optional: JavaScript for the quiz and smooth scrolling
- GitHub Pages for hosting

## 7. Functional Requirements
- Functional level test with instant result display.  
- External social links open in new tabs.
- Image gallery with lightbox for student photos.
- Fully responsive (mobile, tablet, desktop).  
- Cross-browser compatible (Chrome, Safari, Firefox).  

## 8. Accessibility / UX Notes

- Alt text for all images
- High contrast for text readability
- Simple quiz interface accessible on all screen sizes
